Bio

I'm a PhD student in the Branco lab at the Sainsbury Wellcome Centre . Previously master student and reasearch assistant in the Duguid lab at the University of Edinburgh.

Projects

SciDraw is a free repository of high quality open access scientific drawings that I've created with Alex Harston. SciDraw aim is to make freely available drawings and art work to be used for scientific figures in talks, posters and articles. The content available on SciDraw is shared under a creative commons license by artists and scientists, but the project is sponsored by the Sainsbury Wellcome Centre and the Wellcome Trust.

@scidrawio scidraw.io

BrainRender is a python for the creation of 3D interactive renderings of mouse brain anatomical and morphological data. It interacts with the API for the Mouse Connectivity Map and Allen Brain Map projects from the Allen Institute to display anatomical and connectivity data. It also supports the rendering of 3D morphological reconstructions of over a 1000 neurons crated by the Mouse Light project at Janelia.

Research

I'n my PhD I aim to combine innate threat responses with spatial navigation to investigate decision making strategy in rodents. I have developed a novel behavioural task aims to facilitate the detailed description of how environmental factors influence decision making strategy selection at a behavioural level. This, in turn, will lead to the formulation of specific testable hypotheses about the neuronal and computational mechanisms underlying this complex behaviour.
