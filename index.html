My ambition is to remove the bottlenecks between humans and technology. To develop new technology and algorithms to understand user intentions and controll intelligent systems enacting the desired action. Offering a seamless, natural, interface between your thoughts and the world.

Skills: Experimental and Computational Neuroscience - Machine Learning - Deep Learning model design and development - Control Theory - Software development - DataAnalysis - 3D design and manufacturing.


During my PhD I used Reinforcement Learning and quantitative behavioral analysis to understand animals' decision making. I used Control Theory and Neural Decoding to study motor control and its neural bases. I developed an open-source 3D interactive neuroanatomy software used by dozens of labs across the world.

In my Postdoc at MIT, I am developing a novel Deep Learning architecture for Neural Decoding for Brain Computer Interfaces that is both more accurate and efficient than other state-of-the-art methods.
